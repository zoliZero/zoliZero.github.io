How your LabMate will operate

Your tailored LabMate will be linked from your sites. Your clients and colleagues can reach your LabMate through a webbrowser.

  • Demo of LabMate

    Interested on more details? Please, book a LabMate demo by clicking on the following link: [HubSpot meeting link]

  • Ordering LabMate

    Please contact LabShare sales through your direct contact or through the central communication channels highlighted on LabShare website.

  • Tailoring of LabMate - design

    LabMate set up your company’s design and visual appearance. LabMate implements your logo, color, and information into the system to your preferences.

  • Tailoring of LabMate – functionality

    LabMate is a tailored test booking and capacity management system. LabMate provides the engine of LabShare: testing, certification, and consultancy services according to your preferences.

  • Tailoring of LabMate – database

    The LabMate database is tailored to your needs. You can set up your capabilities, can open for the whole LabShare network, or can be limited to preferred companies.

  • Tailoring LabMate – legal conditions

    Operation of LabMate is a duty of LabShare. Your company’s terms and conditions and other legal conditions supporting your business activity can be displayed in the system.

  • Maintenance and updates

    LabShare is responsible for the maintenance of the LabMate system and doing time-by-time updates based on market feedback and trends.

  • Security of LabMate

    LabMate is built on the engine of LabShare. The cloud-based software is tested and verified against penetration and multiple attack cases. The software validation was done by an accredited cyber security laboratory, following the OWASP Top10 recommendations.

  • LabMate subscription

    Subscription is based on the individual contract between LabShare and the Client. Monthly and annual payments can be applied.
